ScoutBall
FIFA World Cup 2022

An interactive football scouting dashboard built for the FIFA World Cup 2022 dataset. Filter players by position and performance metrics, compare up to three players on a radar chart, explore global player distribution on a choropleth map, and dive into multi-dimensional stats via parallel coordinates. Built as a group project at TU/e.
